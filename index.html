iOS 15 Overflow Issue Example

iOS 15 appears to have an issue with overflowing when the Tab Bar is positioned at the top instead of the—new default—bottom.

Steps to reproduce:

  1. Set the Tab Bar position to the top by tap the AA icon and selecting Show Top Address Bar.
  2. Open the modal by scrolling down this page and tapping Open modal.
  3. Scroll down to the bottom of the modal which will hide Safaris UI.
  4. Try clicking the Close button.

You should find that Safari fights with your interaction, showing the bottom bar making it impossible to interact with the close button.

This issue doesn't seem to happen when the Tab Bar is positioned at the bottom of the screen.
